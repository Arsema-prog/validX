## <img src="https://cdn.simpleicons.org/handshake/00A0DC" width="24" height="24" align="center"/> Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an **open and welcoming environment**, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in **ValidX** a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:

<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%">

◆ Age  
◆ Body size  
◆ Disability  
◆ Ethnicity  
◆ Gender identity and expression

</td>
<td width="50%">

◆ Level of experience  
◆ Nationality  
◆ Personal appearance  
◆ Race, religion, or sexual identity  
◆ Any other protected characteristic

</td>
</tr>
</table>

## <img src="https://cdn.simpleicons.org/security/0078D4" width="24" height="24" align="center"/> Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

| Responsibility | Description |
|----------------|-------------|
| **Clarifying Standards** | Defining and communicating acceptable behavior |
| **Taking Action** | Responding appropriately to unacceptable behavior |
| **Content Moderation** | Removing, editing, or rejecting contributions that violate this Code |
| **Fair Investigation** | Reviewing all complaints promptly and fairly |

> [!NOTE]
> Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## <img src="https://cdn.simpleicons.org/minutemailer/0078D4" width="24" height="24" align="center"/> Reporting

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team at:
>
> **<img src="https://cdn.simpleicons.org/maildotru/005FF9" width="18" height="18" align="center"/> arsema.tesfaye@aastustudent.edu.et**
>
> All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality regarding the reporter.

### What to Include in Your Report

◆ Your contact information  
◆ Names of individuals involved  
◆ Description of the incident  
◆ Date and time of occurrence  
◆ Any supporting evidence (screenshots, links, etc.)

## <img src="https://cdn.simpleicons.org/scales/000000" width="24" height="24" align="center"/> Enforcement

> [!WARNING]
> Participants asked to stop any inappropriate behavior are expected to **comply immediately**.

### Consequences

If a participant engages in unacceptable behavior, project maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including:

| Level | Action |
|-------|--------|
| **1** | Written warning with explanation of violation |
| **2** | Temporary suspension from project participation |
| **3** | Permanent expulsion from the project |

## <img src="https://cdn.simpleicons.org/googlemaps/4285F4" width="24" height="24" align="center"/> Scope

This Code of Conduct applies:

▸ **Within project spaces** — GitHub repositories, issue trackers, pull requests  
▸ **In public spaces** — When representing the project or community  
▸ **At events** — Conferences, meetups, or other ValidX-related gatherings

Examples of representing the project include:
- Using an official project email address
- Posting via official social media accounts
- Acting as an appointed representative at events
